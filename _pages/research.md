## Research Overview / Visão Geral da Pesquisa

**English:** My research focuses on **non-perturbative aspects of quantum field theory**, at the intersection of strongly coupled gauge theories and quantum information science.

**Português:** Minha pesquisa concentra-se nos **aspectos não-perturbativos da teoria quântica de campos**, na interface entre as teorias de calibre fortemente acopladas e a ciência da informação quântica.

---

## Research Lines / Linhas de Pesquisa

### 1. Bell Inequalities and Quantum Information in QFT

**Desigualdades de Bell e Informação Quântica em TQC**

We develop a systematic framework for studying Bell-CHSH and Mermin inequalities in relativistic quantum field theory, using Weyl operators and Tomita-Takesaki modular theory. Our approach:

- Achieves maximal violation (Tsirelson bound) in free scalar field theory
- Extends to gauge theories via BRST-invariant formulation
- Applies numerical methods for interacting theories
- Studies violations in various spacetime regions (Rindler wedges, causal diamonds)

_Key tools: Weyl operators, Tomita-Takesaki theory, von Neumann algebras (type III₁)_

---

### 2. Relative Entropy and Entanglement Measures in QFT

**Entropia Relativa e Medidas de Emaranhamento em TQC**

We investigate Araki-Uhlmann relative entropy between quantum states (coherent, squeezed, cat states) in relativistic QFT. This provides rigorous information-theoretic measures of distinguishability between quantum field states, with applications to understanding the vacuum structure of field theories.

---

### 3. Embezzlement of Entanglement in QFT

**Embezzlement de Emaranhamento em TQC**

A new research direction: we study entanglement embezzlement protocols in quantum field theory, leveraging the type III₁ character of local von Neumann algebras. This connects to deep questions about the classification of operator algebras and quantum resource theory.

---

### 4. Confinement and the Gribov Problem

**Confinamento e o Problema de Gribov**

We study colour confinement in QCD through the Gribov-Zwanziger approach to gauge fixing, developing BRST-invariant formulations and studying the infrared behavior of gluon and ghost propagators. This work connects directly to the Clay Millennium Problem of Yang-Mills mass gap.

_Key framework: Gribov-Zwanziger theory, Refined Gribov-Zwanziger, BRST symmetry_

---

### 5. Bell Inequalities as Probes of Confinement

**Desigualdades de Bell como Sondas do Confinamento**

We are developing methods to use entanglement measures and Bell inequalities as order parameters for confinement in gauge theories — connecting our two main research lines in a unified framework using the BRST-invariant formalism.

---

### 6. Modular Theory and Generalized Symmetries

**Teoria Modular e Simetrias Generalizadas**

We investigate connections between Tomita-Takesaki modular theory and the recently developed framework of generalized symmetries (higher-form symmetries), which has revolutionized understanding of QFT phases.

---

### 7. Phases of Gauge Theories

**Fases das Teorias de Calibre**

Study of confinement, Higgs, and Coulomb phases in Yang-Mills theories through spectral functions of gauge-invariant composite operators, with extension to the supersymmetric case.

---

### 8. Topological Materials and Condensed Matter

**Materiais Topológicos e Matéria Condensada**

Application of quantum field theory methods to topological insulators, Weyl semimetals, and topological superconductors, including the role of anomalies and Julia-Toulouse mechanisms.
