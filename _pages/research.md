## Research Overview / Visão Geral da Pesquisa

**English:** My research investigates non-perturbative structures in quantum field theory (QFT), with emphasis on gauge theories, confinement, and quantum-information observables in relativistic settings.

**Português:** Minha pesquisa investiga estruturas não-perturbativas em teoria quântica de campos (TQC), com ênfase em teorias de calibre, confinamento e observáveis de informação quântica em cenários relativísticos.

## Main Research Axes / Eixos Principais

### 1) Quantum Information in Relativistic QFT

- Bell-CHSH and Mermin inequalities in free and interacting field models.
- Local algebras, modular operators, and Tomita-Takesaki methods.
- Relative entropy, distinguishability, and entanglement diagnostics.
- Embezzlement protocols and resource-theoretic structures in type-III algebras.

### 2) Confinement and Infrared Dynamics in Gauge Theories

- Gribov problem and Gribov–Zwanziger framework.
- BRST-invariant formulations and gauge-fixing subtleties.
- Infrared behavior of propagators and gauge-invariant observables.
- Confinement/Higgs/Coulomb phases and phase-sensitive diagnostics.

### 3) Interfaces with Topology and Condensed Matter

- Effective field-theory descriptions for topological phases.
- Topological insulators, Weyl semimetals, and anomaly-related effects.
- Julia–Toulouse-type mechanisms and defect condensation.

## Current Directions / Direções Atuais

- Bell observables as probes of confinement in gauge sectors.
- Modular-theoretic tools for generalized symmetries.
- Numerical platforms for quantum-information observables in QFT.
- Information-theoretic constraints for strongly coupled systems.
