## Syllabus

Mathematical methods for physics: Fourier series, Fourier and Laplace transforms, partial differential equations, special functions, Green's functions, advanced vector analysis.

## Bibliography

- G. B. Arfken, H. J. Weber & F. E. Harris, *Mathematical Methods for Physicists*, Elsevier (2012)
- M. L. Boas, *Mathematical Methods in the Physical Sciences*, Wiley (2005)
