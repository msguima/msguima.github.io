## Syllabus

Introduction to quantum field theory. Canonical quantization of the scalar field, Dirac field, and electromagnetic field. Propagators. Interactions and Feynman diagrams. Cross-section calculations in QED.

## Bibliography

- M. E. Peskin & D. V. Schroeder, *An Introduction to Quantum Field Theory*, Westview Press (1995)
- M. D. Schwartz, *Quantum Field Theory and the Standard Model*, Cambridge (2014)
- S. Weinberg, *The Quantum Theory of Fields, Vol. I*, Cambridge (1995)
