<div class="research-theme">

### Confinement & Gauge Theories

The **Gribov problem** in non-abelian gauge theories and the **Refined Gribov–Zwanziger (RGZ)** approach to confinement in QCD. Topics include: exact nilpotent BRST symmetry, extension to linear covariant gauges, glueball mass calculations and spectral functions, gluon and ghost propagators.

**Keywords:** non-perturbative QCD, Gribov copies, Gribov horizon, confining propagators, BRST

</div>

<div class="research-theme">

### Topological Phases & Defects

Topological order, defect condensation via the Julia–Toulouse mechanism, and Chern–Simons theories. Applications to topological insulators, Weyl superconductors, and monopole operators in topological matter. Multivalued fields and disorder operators.

**Keywords:** topological defects, duality, Julia–Toulouse mechanism, topological insulators

</div>

<div class="research-theme">

### Quantum Information in Quantum Field Theory

Most recent and rapidly growing line of research (21 publications in 2023–2026). Development of an original formalism based on **Weyl operators** and **Tomita–Takesaki modular theory** for the study of:

- **Bell-CHSH** and **Mermin** inequality violations in relativistic QFT
- **Araki–Uhlmann relative entropy** in coherent and squeezed states
- Entanglement measures in free and gauge field theories
- Quantum **embezzlement** in Type III$_1$ von Neumann algebras

**Keywords:** entanglement, Bell inequalities, relative entropy, von Neumann algebras, Tomita–Takesaki

</div>

<div class="research-theme">

### Condensed Matter Applications

Effective field theories for topological superconductors, Lorentz symmetry violation, generalized symmetries. Microscopic fermionic models for Weyl superconductors and the connection between the massive photon propagator and axionic fluctuations.

**Keywords:** topological superconductors, anomalies, generalized symmetries

</div>
