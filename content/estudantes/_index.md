## Current Opportunities

I have openings for **undergraduate research (PIBIC)**, **M.Sc.**, and **Ph.D.** supervision in the following areas:

1. **Quantum information in quantum field theory** — Bell inequalities, entanglement entropy, relative entropy
2. **Non-perturbative aspects of gauge theories** — Gribov problem, confinement, spectral functions
3. **Entanglement in many-body systems** — spin chains, lattice models

Interested students should contact me by email: [msguimaraes@uerj.br](mailto:msguimaraes@uerj.br)

## Information for Applicants

### Undergraduate Research (IC / PIBIC)
- **Prerequisites:** completed Quantum Mechanics I; interest in theoretical physics
- **How to apply:** send an email with your transcript and a brief description of your interests

### M.Sc. / Ph.D.
- **Programme:** [PGFIS — Graduate Programme in Physics, UERJ](https://www.if.uerj.br/pos-graduacao/)
- **Admission:** via [Unified Graduate Entrance Exam (UNIPOSRIO)](http://uniposrio-fisica.cbpf.br/)
- **Scholarships:** CAPES, FAPERJ, CNPq
- **Recommended preparation:** solid background in quantum mechanics, statistical mechanics, and electromagnetism
