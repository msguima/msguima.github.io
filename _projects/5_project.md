## Overview

Many quantities in quantum field theory — entanglement entropy, Bell inequality violations, relative entropy — require sophisticated numerical methods when analytical results are unavailable or insufficient.

This project develops a **computational platform** for evaluating such quantities, with a focus on free scalar and vector quantum field theories.

## Methods

- Spectral decomposition of modular Hamiltonians on discretized spacetime lattices
- Numerical evaluation of the Araki-Uhlmann relative entropy
- Computation of Bell-CHSH expectation values for generic Weyl operators
- Extension to interacting theories via perturbative and lattice approaches
