## Overview

Entanglement entropy and relative entropy are powerful probes of the structure of quantum field theory. This project develops and applies the **Araki-Uhlmann relative entropy** as a measure of distinguishability between states localized in spacetime regions.

## Key Results

- Analytical computation of relative entropy between coherent states in free scalar QFT
- Study of mass dependence of relative entropy across spacetime dimensions
- Relative entropy for single-mode squeezed states
- Numerical analysis of Araki-Uhlmann relative entropy using spectral methods

## Technical Tools

- Araki-Uhlmann relative entropy in von Neumann algebras
- Coherent and squeezed state excitations of the vacuum
- Modular Hamiltonian and Tomita-Takesaki theory
