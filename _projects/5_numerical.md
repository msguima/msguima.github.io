## Overview

Many quantities of interest in non-perturbative QFT—modular Hamiltonians, Bell violation parameters, gluon propagators—are not analytically tractable. This project develops and applies numerical methods to compute these quantities beyond analytic reach.

## Key Results

- Numerical diagonalisation of modular Hamiltonians for massive scalar fields in Rindler wedges
- Numerical platform for computing Bell–CHSH violations as a function of field mass and region geometry
- Comparison of RGZ gluon propagators with unquenched lattice QCD data
- Numerical study of entanglement entropy scaling and area laws in free QFT

## Technical Tools

- Python/NumPy/SciPy for matrix diagonalisation
- Lattice QCD data analysis
- Finite-element methods for operator spectra
