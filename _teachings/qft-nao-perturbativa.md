## Ementa / Syllabus

This graduate course covers non-perturbative methods in quantum field theory, with emphasis on colour confinement and gauge fixing beyond the perturbative regime.

### Topics

1. Review of perturbative QFT and its limitations
2. Non-perturbative phenomena: confinement, chiral symmetry breaking, instantons
3. Lattice QFT: formulation and selected results
4. Gauge fixing in non-Abelian theories: the Faddeev–Popov procedure
5. Gribov copies and the Gribov horizon
6. The Gribov–Zwanziger action
7. The Refined Gribov–Zwanziger framework and dimension-2 condensates
8. Gluon and ghost propagators: theory vs. lattice
9. BRST symmetry and its breaking in the GZ framework
10. Finite-temperature effects and the deconfinement transition

## Prerequisites

- Quantum field theory (at the level of Peskin & Schroeder)
- Basic group theory (SU(N))
